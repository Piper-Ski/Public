The Public Portfolio Repository contains my creative portfolio of work and PMI standard projects.

Projects: 
1: Stakeholder Matrix App 
  Contents: PMI_Stakeholder_Matrix_Plotter.html, Stakeholder Engagement Planning Workbook.pdf
  Instructions: Use the app tool alongside Section 3 of the Workbook or as a stand alone tool. Workbook with app instructions are below.

  Step 1: Identify all Stakeholder Groups in Section 1 | Stakeholder Identification. 
  Step 2: Examine all Stakeholders in Section 2 | Stakeholder Examination.
  Step 3: Classify and prioritize Stakeholders in Section 3 | Stakeholder Classification & Prioritization and Plot Stakeholders in App. Rate each shareholder on a scale of 1-10, with 1 being the lowest and 10 being the highest. For matrix 3.5, fill out the table using the definitions of engagement from the column titles in the Engagement Assessment in the app.
  Step 4: In the app, add all stakeholders under the Add Stakeholder section. The screen to the right plots each stakeholder to the Power/Interest Grid, Influence/Interest Grid, Engagement Assessment, and Salience Model with definitions and classifications provided in keys on the bottom of the screen. 
  Step 5: Return to Sections 3.2, 3.4, & 3.5 and mark an X or the recommended action in the approperiate column for each stakeholder, copying the strategy recommendation back into your Section 3 tables in the workbook. 
 Step 6: Manage and Monitor stakeholders in Section 4 | Stakeholder Management & Monitoring. Use the Engagement Assessment Matrix gap analysis to populate your 4.3 Expectation Management table in Section 4.
 Step 7: Trace governance in Section 5 | Governance Traceability.
 End of Project 1
